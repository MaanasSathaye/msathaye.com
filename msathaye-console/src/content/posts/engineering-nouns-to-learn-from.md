These are resources I've compiled related to "People, Places, and Things" in software. These will be updated with announcements on BlueSky.

## Databases

- [Database Systems course](https://15445.courses.cs.cmu.edu/fall2024/) from [Andy Pavlo](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~pavlo/) & Co. at CMU
- Phil Eaton's [blog](https://eatonphil.com/) + his [reddit post](https://www.reddit.com/r/databasedevelopment/comments/unj8d1/getting_started_with_database_development/?rdt=35381) on intro to database development
- Auxten Wang's [collection](https://github.com/auxten/go-sqldb/issues/7) of database papers

## Distributed Systems

- Jeff Hodges' [Notes on Distributed Systems for Young Bloods](https://www.somethingsimilar.com/2013/01/14/notes-on-distributed-systems-for-young-bloods/)
- [MIT PDOS'](https://pdos.csail.mit.edu/) research, publications, and [distributed systems course.](https://pdos.csail.mit.edu/6.824/index.html)
- Andrey Satarin's [Testing Distributed Systems](https://asatarin.github.io/testing-distributed-systems/)
- Fly.io's Distributed Systems [Challenge](https://fly.io/dist-sys/1/)
- [SadServers.com](https://sadservers.com/)
